Description

The Problem

Tracking trades is crucial for traders. A very simple data is valueble to improve decision making for the next trades. Spreadsheet can help a trader to manage the data collected, but it takes much time to convert it into valuable information.

Solution

Develop a comprehensive web-based trading journal application. The system will automatically generate a strategy win rate, best date and time the strategy working, what are the common mistakes traders commit, probability of the strategy in the long run, and much more. To help out traders to stop executing mistake trades, the system have a session trading where user have a checklist for a valid trades.

Features

Comprehensive chart performance.

Based from user log, the data will be translated into charts, this includes:

  1. Daily, Weekly, Monthly, and Entire Performance
  2. Strategy Performance
  3. Profit and Loss

Session Trading.

Trader will have a trading checklist based from strategy before taking a trade. Before and after a trading session and taking a trade, the system will check the pyschology state of trader. These data will be used to find an answer why a trade won or loss.

Strategy Management.

Trader will add a checklist of the strategy to ensure each trade are based on rules and not emotions. This will also show how a strategy perform.

Trade Management.

Trade mangement includes managing all trades, the taken trade and ongoing trade.

Account Management.

Trader can manage different trading account. This will show overall account performance and individual account.
